Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about image conversion

What image formats can I convert to JPG?

Our tool supports conversion from PNG, WebP, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and many other image formats. You can convert multiple images at once, even if they are in different formats.

Does converting to JPG reduce quality?

JPG is a lossy format, meaning some quality is lost during compression. However, our tool allows you to adjust the quality setting to find the perfect balance between file size and image quality. At higher quality settings (85-100%), the difference is often negligible.

Will transparent backgrounds be preserved?

JPG format does not support transparency. By default, transparent areas will be converted to white. You can disable the "Remove transparency" option if you want to keep transparent backgrounds, but note they will still become white in the JPG output.

How many images can I convert at once?

There is no set limit to the number of images you can convert at once. However, very large batches (50+ images) or extremely large files may cause performance issues depending on your device's capabilities.
