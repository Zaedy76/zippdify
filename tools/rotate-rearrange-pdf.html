Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PDF rotation

Will rotating pages affect the quality of my PDF?

No, rotating pages preserves the original quality of your PDF. Text, images, and formatting remain exactly as they were in the original file.

Can I rotate just one page in a multi-page PDF?

Yes, you can rotate individual pages. Each page has its own rotation controls that affect only that specific page.

Is there a limit to how many pages I can rotate/rearrange?

There's no fixed limit, but very large PDFs (over 100MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities. For best performance, we recommend files under 50MB.

Are my files secure during processing?

Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are never uploaded to any server. Once you close the browser tab, all file data is permanently deleted.
