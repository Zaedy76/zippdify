Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about splitting PDFs

Can I select non-consecutive pages to extract?

Yes, you can extract any combination of pages. You can select individual pages by clicking on them in the preview, or specify page ranges like "1-3,5,7-9" to extract multiple sections.

Does splitting affect the quality of my PDF?

No, the splitting process preserves the original quality of your PDF. Text, images, and formatting remain exactly as they were in the original file.

Is there a limit to the PDF size I can split?

There's no fixed limit, but very large PDFs (over 100MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities. For best performance, we recommend files under 50MB.

Are my files secure during the splitting process?

Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are never uploaded to any server. Once you close the browser tab, all file data is permanently deleted.
