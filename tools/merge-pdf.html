Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about merging PDFs

Is there a limit to how many PDFs I can merge?

There is no set limit to the number of PDFs you can merge. However, very large numbers of files (100+) or extremely large files may cause performance issues depending on your device's capabilities.

Will the quality of my PDFs be reduced?

No, our merger preserves the original quality of your PDF documents. Images, text, and formatting remain exactly as they were in the original files.

Are my files secure during merging?

Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are never uploaded to any server. Once you close the browser tab, all file data is permanently deleted.

Can I rearrange the order of PDFs?

Yes, you can easily rearrange the order of your PDF files before merging. Simply use the up and down arrows next to each file to change their position in the final merged document.
