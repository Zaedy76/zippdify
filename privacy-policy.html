Introduction

Welcome to Zippdify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure experience when using our online PDF and image processing tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our services.

Important Notice

Zippdify is designed with privacy as a core principle. Unlike many online services, we process all files directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. This means your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

By using Zippdify, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services.

2

Information We Don't Collect

At Zippdify, we believe in minimal data collection. Here's what we do not collect:

  • Personal Information: We don't ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Account Data: Our services don't require registration, so we don't create user accounts or store login credentials.
  • Payment Information: All our tools are completely free, so we never process payments or collect financial information.
  • File Content: Your uploaded files are processed entirely in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Patterns: We don't track which tools you use, how often you use them, or your browsing patterns.

The only information we might temporarily process is:

  1. Basic browser information (user agent) for compatibility purposes
  2. IP address (anonymized and not stored) for basic security monitoring
  3. Cookies only for essential functionality (see Cookie Policy below)

File Processing & Security

This is the most important section for understanding how Zippdify protects your files:

Client-Side Processing Architecture

All file processing occurs directly in your web browser using JavaScript. This client-side architecture provides several security benefits:

  • No Server Uploads: Files are never transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Immediate Deletion: Once you close the browser tab or refresh the page, all file data is permanently deleted from memory.
  • End-to-End Privacy: Your files never leave the privacy of your own device.
  • No Storage: We don't have servers to store your files, so there's no risk of data breaches involving your documents.

Security Features

Memory-Only Processing: Files are loaded into your browser's memory (RAM) for processing and are never written to disk on our systems. When the processing is complete, the memory is released.

No Backups: Since files aren't stored, there are no backups or archives containing your data.

SSL Encryption: Our website uses HTTPS with SSL encryption to secure the transmission of the webpage itself, though file data doesn't need to be transmitted.

Technical Implementation

We use modern web technologies that operate entirely within the browser sandbox:

  • JavaScript Libraries: Tools like PDF-Lib.js, jsPDF, and Canvas API run locally in your browser.
  • Web Workers: Heavy processing is handled in separate threads to prevent browser freezing.
  • Memory Management: We implement proper memory cleanup to ensure no residual data remains.

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality only:

Essential Cookies

  • Theme Preference: Stores your dark/light mode preference locally on your device.
  • Session Management: Basic session cookies that expire when you close your browser.

What We Don't Use

  • No Tracking Cookies: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking cookies.
  • No Advertising Cookies: We don't serve ads, so there are no advertising cookies.
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We don't embed content that would set third-party cookies.

Browser Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not cookies) for:

  • Remembering your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Temporarily storing file lists during processing (deleted when you leave the page)

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings, and it won't affect the functionality of our tools.

Third-Party Services

Zippdify uses a few third-party services for essential functionality:

Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

We use CDNs to deliver library files for better performance:

  • Font Awesome: For icons (loaded from cdnjs.cloudflare.com)
  • JavaScript Libraries: PDF-Lib, jsPDF, QRCode.js (loaded from cdnjs.cloudflare.com)
  • Fonts: Web fonts for typography

These CDNs may collect basic usage statistics (like any website visit), but they don't receive any information about your files or specific tool usage.

No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user information with third parties for their commercial purposes. Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to share.

External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.
